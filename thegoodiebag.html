The Goodie Bag

The Goodie Bag is a candy distribution company located in Chicago, IL, that sells lesser-known candy products and different flavors of traditional candy products. It discovers countless regional candies and variations of well known candies within the United States, making them available to consumers throughout the country and encouraging them to explore something new. Without a brick-and-mortar presense, the company operates by shipping candy products from its centrally-located warehouse. I designed a logo, email signature, stationery, menu, uniform, packaging, and environmental, fleet and wayfinding signage to give the brand a consistent visual identity that supports its mission.

Visual Identity

Although traditional candy brands tend to have bold and playful identities, I wanted The Goodie Bag's brand aesthetic to appear more elegant and professional, while still creating the happy feeling that is typically associated with candy.
