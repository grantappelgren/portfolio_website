LEVEL 10

About the Project

Level 10 is a fictional DJ (disk jockey). For a college project, I creating Branding and Identity, Merchandise and Marketing designs for the music artist. Pre-production was required to include a mood board and color scheme, with production needing to include logos, stickers, t-shirts, flyer and poster designs. The objective was to design and develop a brand for a new artist, promote the artist and advertise for an upcoming performance.

Visual Identity

Level 10 produces electronic dance music, which is known to be quite loud. That said, I wanted to ensure branding elements were designed to incorporate the ideas of pulsating music and rising or increasing volume. It was important to highlight the notion of a traditional number scale, with 10 being the peak.

Visual Design

Visuals used throughout the designs are meant to represent consistency and include an elevator, elevator buttons, commercial floor numbering, sound waves, a quickly-increasing bar chart, and sound dials. Each of these are meant to reflect the theme of arriving at a higher level. A couple designs (male and female t-shirts) purposely do not include the complete name of the artist, but instead, rely on the communication of meaningful symbolism that is synonymous with the artist's name. Colors were chosen to both foster contrast and allow for gradations when desired.
