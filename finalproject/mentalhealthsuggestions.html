MENTAL HEALTH SUGGESTIONS

About the Project

Mental Health Suggestions are healthy habits that can be incorporated into an individual's day-to-day life to specifically combat the mental health disorder known as depression. For a college project, I designed a full-color infographic meant to educate the public about possible ways to overcome this mental obstacle. The infographic was required to be created in Illustrator and include icons from the free open-source database, Noun Project. The objective was to design an appropriate and well-executed infographic that could be displayed in a proper setting such as a medical clinic, hospital, etc. Given the serious and sensitive subject-matter, it was expecially important to be cognizant of how the infographic would be perceived by the audience.

Visual Identity

Depression is something that can have a major impact on a person's life. Its severity depends on the individual, but understanding the urgency of addressing it before it worsens cannot be understated. In a setting where patients are looking for advice or remedies for a medical condition, visuals like infographics should not bring about an uncomfortable feeling. The design needed to be informative, while incorporating encouragement and having a calming visual appeal.

Visual Design

The infographic was designed to have a calming influence on the viewer, with a concept that was straightforward and easy to comprehend. By using thought bubbles paired with recognizable icons, there is a rudimentary element that posters and infographics often portray in a medical setting. I was careful to use rounded shapes such as clouds and circles to further exemplify this. Direction and separation between clouds is developed through paths created from gradated circles. Soft and light colors were selected to portray clouds and thinking.
