ECHO DROP

About the Project

Echo Drop is a fictional electronic dance music festival. Its lineup features artists that purely play the music genre of trance. For a college project, I designed a full-color poster for the event. The poster was required to include illustrations created in Illustrator, typography, headline phrase, dates, location, etc. The objective was to create an interesting, appropriate, eye-catching and well-executed advertisement.

Visual Identity

Trance is a distinctly repetitive form of music that relies on a beat reminiscent of echoes. Many of the tracks build up to eventual "drops" that are considered peaks or culmination points. The design needed to incorporate these audio descriptions and express them in a unique visual way.

Visual Design

The purpose of the poster was to provide intrigue and be a fun representation of the festival. By using water droplets falling towards a booming stereo, I was able to create a design that fits the festival name and nature of the music. Inspired by the film TRON, I chose to use an electronic-looking typeface to go along with the idea that you are seemingly transported to another world while listening to trance.
