Hi,
I'm Grant

I'm a current college student in the process of earning an AA in Graphic & Interactive Design from Foothill College as I transition my career from Finance to Design. I hold a B.A in Economics, a Minor in Business, and have over 5 years of work experience as a Financial Analyst with companies including Apple and Grove Collaborative.  I'm open to roles and projects in Graphic Design and UI/UX Design & Development.


Designer Statement


My work consists of Branding, Marketing and Advertising, UI/UX and Website Design. One of the most gratifying feelings comes from being able to conceptualize an idea, lay the groundwork of its design, and develop it into reality. The level of appreciation I have for the entire process is just as great as that of the final product, making each step of a project enjoyable. With every project, the challenge is to create solutions that sit at the intersection of creativity and intuition. Elements and principles of design are carefully infused with common understandings to arrive at a balanced visual expression. Coming from an economics and finance background, it is natural for me to view a design from a cost/benefit perspective. I want to ensure my designs are only additive and do not take away from the messages being communicated. Approaching a design through that lens allows me to focus on elements that are truly important and thereby avoid unnecessary components and over-designing.


My inspiration stems from knowing that I not only can create something that looks/operates great, but also that my work can positively impact another person or organization. I realize my designs may improve a business’ brand and foot traffic, inform the public about an event or healthy habits, provide ways to check on, learn about, and interact with content digitally, etc. It is humbling to both have a passion for the work and understand that I have an opportunity to make a difference.
