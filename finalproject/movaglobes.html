MOVA GLOBES

About the Project

Mova Globes is a real-world company that manufactures naturally-rotating acrylic globes. Using just solar energy and earth's magnetic field, the product is able to operate without any cords or batteries. For a college project, I designed two full-color advertisements promoting a Black Friday Sale. One formatted vertically and the other horizontally, in the form of a banner. The advertisements were required to include an image edited in Photoshop, typography, company name, hyperlinks, etc. The objective was to create and intriguing, eye-catching and well-executed advertisements that encouraged action and generated traffic.

Visual Identity

Mova Globes separates itself from other manufacturers with its state-of-the-art automatic rotating technology. However, this is nearly impossible to display in a static advertisement without animation. This capability is not the only element that sets this product apart from the competition. The high-end design, material, and overall aesthetic stands out as well. The advertisements needed to strongly reflect this luxurious quality through proper imagery, color and description.

Visual Design

With the main purpose of advertising a sale, it was important to ensure that there was a healthy balance between designing for the sale event and designing for the product itself. Incorporating a red ribbon containing text that announces the deal creates a contrast with the rest of the design and grabs the viewers attention. Similarly, blue links are meant to draw clicks and drive viewer interaction and traffic. The globe image displays the sleek product design and clean acrylic. Of the many globe offerings, I chose to showcase a gold option, pairing it with gold typeface throughout the advertisement designs. Gold represents luxury, wealth, and elegance, and with a black background, those emotions are elevated even further.
