MOVA GLOBES

About the Project

Mova Globes is a real-world company that manufactures naturally-rotating acrylic globes. Using just solar energy and earth's magnetic field, the product is able to operate without any cords or batteries. For a college project, I designed two full-color advertisements promoting a Black Friday Sale. One formatted vertically and the other horizontally, in the form of a banner. The advertisements were required to include an image edited in Photoshop, typography, the company name, hyperlinks, etc. The objective was to create intriguing, eye-catching, and well-executed advertisements that encouraged action and generated traffic.

Visual Identity

Mova Globes separates itself from other manufacturers with its state-of-the-art automatic rotating technology. However, this is nearly impossible to display in a static advertisement without animation. This capability is not the only element that sets this product apart from the competition, however. The high-end design, material, and overall aesthetic stands out as well. The advertisements needed to strongly reflect this luxurious quality through proper imagery, color and description.

Typography

I wanted an appropriate typeface to go with the holiday season. Black Friday is synonymous with that time of year, so displaying it using Fireplace gives it a classy thematic representation. Arial Black is a clean and bold sans serif typeface that works great for attention-grabbing headlines and action elements. To develop a sophisticated aesthetic that matches the brand, the serif typeface, Minion Pro, is used for text describing the product.

Colors

#E0A753

Knowing that the advertisement backgrounds were going to be black, selecting bright colors that would offer contrast was imperative. Red is a color that demands attention and highlights important elements of a design. For an advertisement of a sale, it is a perfect color. An almost neon blue stands out as well, but is more subtle than red. Blue is consistently used in design for underlined links, and with a link to the company page as part of the advertisement, a blue color was needed. Gold represents luxury, wealth, and elegance, and with the black background, I wanted those emotions to be elevated even further.

Advertisements

With the main purpose of advertising a sale event, it was important to ensure that there was a healthy balance between designing for both the event and the product itself. Incorporating a red ribbon containing text that announces the deal creates a contrast with the rest of the design and grabs the viewer's attention. Similarly, blue links are meant to draw clicks and drive viewer interaction and traffic. The globe image displays the sleek product design and clean acrylic. A black background was chosen to allow for the image to pop off the page and appear 3-D. Of the many globe offerings, I chose to showcase a gold option, pairing it with gold typeface throughout the advertisement designs and demonstrating repetition.
