E-COMMERCE

About the Project

E-commerce is a web-application product-page mockup for a camera company's online store. For a case study assigned as part of a job-application for a web designer role, I was tasked with creating a mockup for a product-page of a camera company's website. The mockup was required to contain sections for a navigation menu, five hero images, an item description, customer reviews, shipping and warranty information, an option to subscribe to the company newsletter, and a footer. The design needed to be unique, but not too complex for developers to create.

Visual Identity

The photography industry is very professional, with cameras and associated equipment being extremely fragile and nuanced. To align with this, I wanted the page design to be clean, sharp, and serious. Realizing that there would be a significant amount of text spread throughout the page, applying balance, separation, and visual hierarchy was going to be important. Rather than displaying product images in a basic format, I viewed this as an opportunity to incorporate interaction, movement, and further implement an elegant look-and-feel to the page.

Visual Design

A split 2-column design that condenses down to 1-column as the user's screen size decreases allows for a balanced page. The color scheme purposely consists of only a few colors, with a soft blue used to highlight buttons and certain small design elements. Horizontal lines are placed beneath section titles to create separation and consistency. The typeface, Biko, adds character to the page without taking away from the intended professional aesthetic. A rotating carousel for product hero images brings animation and smooth consistency that is reminiscent of an automobile show-room display. To give depth to the carousel, image opacity decreases as it moves further away. The product-page mockup effectively meets the case study specifications and provides a nice foundation that other pages of the web-application can follow to create a cohesive online shopping experience.
