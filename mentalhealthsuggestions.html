Mental Health Suggestions

Mental Health Suggestions are healthy habits that can be incorporated into an individual's day-to-day life to specifically combat the mental health disorder, depression. I designed a full-color infographic meant to educate the public about possible ways to overcome this mental obstacle. The objective was to design an appropriate and well-executed infographic that could be displayed in a proper setting such as a medical clinic, hospital, etc.

Visual Identity

In a setting where patients are looking for advice or remedies for a medical condition, visuals like infographics should bring about a comfortable feeling. The design needed to be informative, while incorporating encouragement and having a calming visual appeal.

Typography

To not interfere with or foster unintended feelings, I selected a basic typeface to display the text content. "Helvetica Nueu" is a legible and neutral typeface allowing the other design elements to carry more meaning.

Colors

Soft colors were selected to portray calmness and alleviate pressure, with different blues creating a refreshing and healthy image.

Infographic

By using thought bubbles paired with recognizable icons, there is a rudimentary element that posters and infographics often portray in a medical setting. Simple, rounded shapes such as clouds and circles exemplify this.
