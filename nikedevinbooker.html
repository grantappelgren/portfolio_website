Nike - Devin Booker

One of the many athletes Nike has a partnership with is professional basketball player, Devin Booker. It was recently announced that Booker will be receiving his own signature model Nike basketball shoe in 2023. As a passion project, I wanted to create a marketing campaign to bring additional awareness to Booker's athletic and mental prowess, building excitement around the shoe release.

Visual Identity

Signature shoe deals are reserved for basketball's biggest and most marketable stars. It is the pinnacle of showcasing an athlete's personal brand, combining their design preferences with their name and overall appearance. Devin Booker is not only recognized for his athletic greatness on the court, but also his mentality. That said, the marketing campaign needed to feature both outstanding photography and significant text that represents his performance, inspiration, and story. Ensuring he is depicted and positioned as an admirable figure to the public was imperative. All campaign assets would be on-brand with Nike's advertising approach and communicate that Booker is not only likable and easy to root for, but an individual who is relatable and someone people want to be like.
