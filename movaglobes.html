Mova Globes

Mova Globes is a company that manufactures naturally-rotating, acrylic globes. Using just solar energy and earth's magnetic field, the product is able to operate without any cords or batteries. I designed two web advertisements promoting a Black Friday Sale; one vertical (email) and one horizontal (web/social-media banner). The objective was to create intriguing, eye-catching and well-executed advertisements that encouraged action and generated traffic.

Visual Identity

Mova Globes' automatic rotating technology is not the only element that sets their products apart from the competition. The high-end design, material, and overall aesthetic stands out as well. The advertisements needed to strongly reflect this luxurious quality through proper imagery, color and description.

Typography & Colors

I wanted an appropriate typeface to go with the holiday season. Black Friday is synonymous with that time of year, so displaying it using "Fireplace" gives a classy thematic representation. "Arial Black" is a clean and bold sans serif typeface that works great for text along curved paths and attention-grabbing headlines. Knowing that the advertisement backgrounds were going to be black, selecting bright colors that would offer contrast was imperative. Red is a color that demands attention and highlights important elements of a design, which is perfect for a sale advertisement. Gold represents luxury, wealth, and elegance, and along with black, it elevates those emotions even further.
