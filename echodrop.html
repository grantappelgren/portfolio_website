Echo Drop

Echo Drop is an Electronic Dance Music festival. Its lineup features artists that purely play the music genre of Trance. I designed a full-color poster for the event. The objective was to produce an interesting, appropriate, eye-catching and well-executed advertisement.

Visual Identity

Trance is a distinctly repetitive form of music that relies on a beat reminiscent of echoes. Many of the tracks build up to eventual "drops" that are considered peaks or culmination points. The design needed to incorporate these audio descriptions and express them in a unique visual way.

Typography & Colors

Inspired by the film TRON, I chose to use an electronic-looking typeface to go along with the idea that you are seemingly transported to another world while listening to Trance. Wanting to use water-droplets in the design, blue colors were selected, along with orange complementary colors for contrast.
