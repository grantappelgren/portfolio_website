ECHO DROP

About the Project

Echo Drop is a fictional Electronic Dance Music festival. Its lineup features artists that purely play the music genre of Trance. I designed a full-color poster for the event. The objective was to produce an interesting, appropriate, eye-catching and well-executed advertisement.

ROLE

Design

CLIENT

Echo Drop

DATE

Nov. 2020

TOOLS

Illustrator

Visual Identity

Trance is a distinctly repetitive form of music that relies on a beat reminiscent of echoes. Many of the tracks build up to eventual "drops" that are considered peaks or culmination points. The design needed to incorporate these audio descriptions and express them in a unique visual way.

Typography

Inspired by the film TRON, I chose to use an electronic-looking typeface to go along with the idea that you are seemingly transported to another world while listening to Trance.

Wanting to use water-droplets in the design, blue colors were selected, along with natural warm orange complementary colors for contrast.

Poster

By using water droplets falling towards a booming stereo, I created a design that fits the festival name and nature of the music. Both water and sound waves reverberate as an echo, while water molecules fall as drops, like the festival's namesake. Combining the two concepts make for a nice marriage of meaning and a unique visualization. Overall, the poster provides intrigue and is a fun representation of the festival.
