Level 10

Level 10 is a new disc jockey (DJ) who plays electronic dance music (EDM). I created logo and visual identity, merchandise and marketing designs to establish the music artist's visual brand, promote their music and advertise for upcoming performances.

Visual Identity

Given the nature of EDM, I wanted to ensure branding elements were designed to incorporate the ideas of pulsating sounds and increasing volume. It was also important to highlight the notion of a traditional number scale, with 10 being the peak.

Typography & Colors

Logos

The primary lolo is a tiled sound wave chart that an amplifier would display when measuring different frequencies. Colors start out dark at the bottom and become brighter as pitches increase. The secondary logo is comprised of elevator buttons highlighting the 10th level, with an upward-pointing arrow stating that the level has been reached from below.
