TEAMCAL AI

About the Project

Teamcal Ai is a meeting automation platform with the goal of creating a seamless meeting experience for teams and companies across time zones. For a case study assigned as part of a job-application, I created a t-shirt design geared towards the marketing campaign for the launch of the platform's Version 2.0. The objective was to highlight the theme of "Got upgraded to 2.0", while using the color and theme in the Teamcal.ai website and thoroughly explaining the thought process behind my design.

Visual Identity

A product upgrade launch is always significant, and marketing its announcement can play a major role in new user adoption and creating excitement amongst existing users. As such, ensuring the campaign logo encompassed visual elements that represent the product was important. I also wanted to incorporate a bit of symbolism to bring more communication through the design's limited space. Ultimately, the text pertaining to the announcement itself needed to be at the forefront.

Typography

To remain consistent with the brand, I used "Montserrat", which is the typeface the company uses for the body of its website.

These colors are used only sparingly throughout the company's website, but are implemented here to convey meetings on a calendar.

Campaign Logo

To bring in the idea of a global product, I created a background depicting a globe. The circular nature of a globe works perfectly for a symmetrical design. Current and target clients are based in all parts of the world, so this aligns with the product’s intended audience. To further epitomize the global aspect of the product, I thought of a creative way to marry a calendar with the globe. Lines of longitude and latitude are used as separators for days of the week and times of the day respectively, ultimately creating a grid that is reminiscent of a typical calendar. Meetings are placed throughout the grid, atop different parts of the globe. This demonstrates how meetings often span several time zones, something that the product helps simplify. I wanted to represent the idea of an upgrade both literally and figuratively. From a literal standpoint, I use text reading “Upgraded”. I incorporate figurative perspective through symbolism in two ways. First, by extending the ascenders of the “U” and “d” in “Upgraded”, they take the form of upward-pointing arrows. This is representative of an increase in features and improvement of the software from the prior version. Second, utilizing the calendar logomark, I decided to portray the metaphor of turning the page to a new month, which translates here to upgrading the current version and revealing the version “2.0”. The existing company logotype is placed below the main design elements to round out the overall design.

T-Shirt

To show the full-breadth of the logo, it's front-and-center on the shirt. This makes it easy to recognize and read without much effort. On the back side, just adding the calendar icon that includes the text "Upgraded" and "2.0" gives a glimpse of what appears on the front. It's subtle, but a detail that has purpose.
