Teamcal Ai

Teamcal Ai is a meeting automation platform with the goal of creating a seamless meeting experience for teams and companies across various time zones. I created design assets meant for the marketing campaign associated with the launch of the platform's Version 2.0.

Visual Identity

A product upgrade launch is always significant, and marketing its announcement can play a major role in new user adoption and creating excitement amongst existing users. As such, ensuring the campaign logo and overall message encompassed both visual and descriptive elements representing the product was important. All campaign material needed to have a consistent tone and appearance to make for an on-brand look-and-feel.

Campaign Guidelines

To bring in the idea of an global product, the logo background depicts a globe. Current and target clients are based in all parts of the world, so this aligns with the product’s intended audience. Lines of longitude and latitude are used as separators for days of the week and times of the day, ultimately creating a grid that is reminiscent of a typical calendar. Meetings are placed throughout the grid, demonstrating how they often span several time zones. Utilizing the calendar logomark, I decided to portray the metaphor of turning the page to a new month, which translates here to upgrading the current version and revealing the version “2.0”.


To remain consistent with the brand, I used "Montserrat", which is the typeface the company uses for the body of its website. The dominant colors I selected for the campaign logo also match those used throughout the company's website. Light red, orange, green and purple are used within the calendar-grid portion of the logo. These colors are seen sparingly throughout the company's website, but are implemented here to convey meetings on a calendar.
